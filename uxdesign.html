Redesigning TikTok Effects - a UX Case Study

Created by Lindsey McKim

September 2020

The first project in my SMAD 308 class, consisted of redesigning a user flow from an app. I chose to redesign the effects feature in TikTok. For the assignment, we had to conduct surveys on users of the app to understand what their needs and goals are. From there, I was able to understand how to best go about redesigning the app. I created a wireframe to understand the architecture of the app and mapped out the before and after user flow. I then went into Photoshop to redesign the app. After completing these tasks, I put all of my information into a coded webpage which you can view by clicking the link above.

PDConnect

Created by Lindsey McKim and Ana Hart

January 2019 - May 2019

For my SMAD 317 class, we were tasked with creating a high fidelity prototype of an app. My partner and I created PDConnect, an app that brings event organizers, volunteers, and especially caregivers of individuals with Parkinson's Disease together into one central hub. To create this app we had to do extensive user research and usability testing. Our professor submitted our work to the AEJMC'S Best of the Web digital competition.
